Drop 8 photos named 01.jpg .. 08.jpg here.

They will appear as defaults in the polaroid wall. The wall has 8 slots
that map to: primera cita, día de playa, la cabina, noche de sushi,
roadtrip, backstage, por café, nuestra canción.

Any photo uploaded via the UI is saved in localStorage and overrides
the default for that slot on that device.

Recommended size: 600x600 or square-ish, under 1MB each.
